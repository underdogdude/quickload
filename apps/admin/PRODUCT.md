# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Quickload staff and operators who monitor production parcel flow, payment state, customer records, pickup slots, and LINE notifications during daily support work.

## Product Purpose

The admin dashboard gives staff one operational source of truth for parcel exceptions, unpaid shipments, carrier status, and customer support follow-up. Success means staff can identify what needs attention, open the right record, and take the next operational action without guessing.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, accountable. The interface should feel like a logistics control room: quiet enough for long sessions, dense enough for repeated operations, and confident enough for production support.

## Anti-references

Do not make this a marketing dashboard, analytics showpiece, decorative SaaS landing page, or colorful toy-like control panel. Avoid oversized hero metrics, decorative gradients, nested cards, novelty navigation, and any UI that makes operational state harder to scan.

## Design Principles

- Surface exceptions first: unpaid, failed, canceled, and missing-price work belongs above passive totals.
- Keep data close to action: every row should expose the tracking identifier and a clear route to the record.
- Use restrained emphasis: color should mean state, priority, or action.
- Make dense information readable: tables, labels, and controls should support repeated staff use.
- Preserve production trust: empty, loading, and error states must be explicit and calm.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Support keyboard navigation, visible focus states, reduced motion, and layouts that remain usable on laptop and mobile widths.
